Community is essential to us at Significa. We believe everyone benefits from collaboration and knowledge sharing, and we're eager to learn from what's happening outside our walls. We foster this spirit in three main ways.

## Open source

We love open source, we use it, and we contribute to the community. After all, it can't be all take, take, take, can it? To learn more about our approach, dive into Open source.

## Conferences

Go out, learn, and share the Significa story with the wider world! If there's a conference you'd like to attend, we'll make it happen. For more details, check out Engineering conferences.

## Developer meetings

Knowledge sharing is key for us. Developer meetings provide a space to bounce ideas off each other, share helpful tips, and see what your peers are working on. To learn more about our approach, check out Developer meetings.
