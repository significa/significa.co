Project management might not be as flashy as design or development, but it's the foundation of everything we do. Without it, projects drag on, details slip through the cracks, and clients end up frustrated. Simple as that.

Start by understanding The Ethos of the project manager and what we expect from our project managers at Significa. Once you've seen what that looks like, you can dive into the specifics of our process:

- Project kick-off sets the stage for our partnership and aligns everyone before we build anything.
- Monitoring Progress throughout design and development to keep projects on track and on time.
- Continuous Delivery processes that get updates into clients' and users' hands as efficiently as possible.
- Quality Assurance processes we follow for every delivery to maintain our quality standards.

After multiple iterations and deliveries, there comes a point where we transition ownership back to the client. We hand over the final deliverables along with thorough documentation so they can manage updates independently going forward.

Project closure is always bittersweet. But once it's complete, we're ready to do it all over again on the next one.
