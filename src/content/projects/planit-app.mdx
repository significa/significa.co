Planning a trip with friends should be exciting, not exhausting. Planit asked us to design a mobile app that turns the chaos of group travel into a shared adventure.

## The problem

Group trips fall apart in WhatsApp threads. Dates get lost, nobody agrees on the hotel, and someone always gets left out of the loop. Planit wanted a purpose-built tool for collaborative trip planning.
