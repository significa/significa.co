Shelf came to us with a bold ambition: build a banking experience that feels nothing like a bank. Over six months, we designed and shipped a product that turned heads in the fintech space.

## The challenge

Traditional banking apps are built around transactions. Shelf wanted to build around goals — helping people understand where their money goes and where it should go next.

## Research and discovery

We started with two weeks of user interviews, talking to 30 people about their relationship with money. The insight that shaped everything: people don't think in transactions, they think in intentions.

## The product

The final product centers around three pillars: clarity, goals, and nudges. Every screen answers one question: what should I do with my money right now?
