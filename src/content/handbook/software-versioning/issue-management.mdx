We don't want to overstep into Project management territory, but from a developer's perspective, it's essential to know that we manage issues in Linear. We separate tasks into 'To Do' and triaged issues, which can include bugs, feature requests, or items that need further planning.

Link your pull requests to an issue when possible; however, we're flexible about this. Collaborate with your teammates to organise your workflow as you see fit — remember, Maximum freedom, maximum responsibility! Linear offers handy features, such as easily copying issue links and IDs and generating branch names.

BTW, features are not bugs.
