The Playbook is our north star. It captures the principles we believe in and the values we hold, guiding every decision we make — from how we hire to how we build products to how we treat each other.

## Good enough is never enough

We set the bar high and keep raising it. Quality is non-negotiable, whether we're writing a line of code, crafting a pixel, or sending an email. We don't ship things we're not proud of.

## Office first

We are an office-first company. The spontaneous conversations, the impromptu whiteboard sessions, the shared lunches — these are the things that build culture and make us who we are. Remote work has its place, but we believe in the power of physical togetherness.

## Maximum freedom, maximum responsibility

We trust our people completely. There are no time trackers, no micromanagers, no hand-holding. In return, we expect full ownership of your work and your commitments. Freedom without accountability is chaos. Responsibility without freedom is a cage. We choose neither.

## Small on purpose

We have no intention of becoming a large agency. Growth for its own sake dilutes quality, culture, and the things that make Significa special. We want to remain close-knit, intentional, and excellent.

## Clients aren't always right — and neither are we

We push back when we disagree. We defend our decisions with data and reasoning. But we stay genuinely open to being wrong. The best outcomes come from honest dialogue, not from either side capitulating.

## People over process

Processes exist to serve people, not the other way around. We adapt, we iterate, and we remove friction wherever we find it. A good process is one nobody notices.
