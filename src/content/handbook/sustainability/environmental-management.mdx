Our commitment to sustainability can't be just easy lunch-time talk. It must be a core part of our identity at Significa, a serious commitment to our planet. We want to ensure this isn't about lofty ideals but tangible actions with measurable impact.

Throughout 2023, we implemented a range of policies to lessen our environmental impact, but in all honestly, we're no experts. We did the basics. In 2024, we teamed up with ESG experts, Inhabit as we looked to do more. We've since measured our Scope 1, 2 and full Scope 3 emissions for 2023 and 2024, and crafted a Carbon Reduction Plan to be our roadmap to measurable, meaningful impact, ensuring that we grow responsibly while reducing our environmental footprint.

## Our commitment

Significa is dedicated to achieving net-zero carbon emissions by 2050 or sooner. In the delivery of our Carbon Reduction Plan we commit to setting a verified net-zero target by 2027.

We are committed to setting a credible carbon reduction target that aligns with the Science Based Targets initiative (SBTi) and the goals of the Paris Agreement to limit global temperature rise to 1.5°C. By adhering to SBTi guidance, we ensure that our emissions reduction efforts are rooted in climate science and contribute meaningfully to the global effort to mitigate climate change.

We're committed to cutting emissions intensity year-on-year, focusing on high-impact areas like supplier engagement, smarter travel choices, and fostering sustainable behaviours within our team. This isn't about ticking boxes - it's about embedding sustainability into everything we do, making sure every decision reflects our responsibility to the planet.

We've already made strides: from implementing a robust recycling system and enhancing e-waste disposal, to subsidising public transport for commuting. But there is more to do. Our next steps include refining our supplier policies, expanding renewable energy use, and continuing to improve data transparency. Sustainability is about constant progress, not a finish line.

We'll be sharing our journey along the way, keeping our community informed and involved. This is a collective effort, and by making sustainability a core part of Significa's identity, we hope to lead by example, in not just reducing our impact, but actively contributing to a better future.
