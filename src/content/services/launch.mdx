## What we do.

The gap between "code complete" and "live in production" is where most projects stumble. Features work in staging but break under real traffic. Content hasn't been migrated. Analytics aren't tracking the right events. Monitoring isn't catching the problems that matter. We close that gap systematically.

<Callout title="Quality assurance">

- **Cross-browser and cross-device testing**
- **Accessibility auditing (WCAG compliance)**
- **Performance benchmarking**
- **Edge case and regression testing**

</Callout>

<Callout title="Operations">

- **Monitoring and alerting (Sentry, uptime)**
- **Content migration and validation**
- **Analytics and event tracking setup**
- **DNS, SSL, and infrastructure cutover**
- **Deployment pipelines and rollback plans**

</Callout>

## Our approach.

Launch is planned from the start, not bolted on at the end. We define success criteria early: what metrics need to be tracked, what uptime SLA is expected, what monitoring needs to be in place before the first user sees the product. By the time we reach launch, the operational foundation is already running.

## Why Significa.

We don't hand off and disappear. The team that built the product is the team that launches it. They know where the complexity lives, what the edge cases are, and what to watch for in the first 48 hours. That continuity is the difference between a smooth launch and a fire drill.
