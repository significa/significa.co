## Our commitment to ethical conduct

At Significa, our Code of Ethics is the foundation of how we operate. It guides our decisions, shapes our relationships, and ensures we maintain the highest standards of integrity in everything we do.

This code applies to everyone at Significa — from leadership to every team member — and governs how we interact with colleagues, clients, partners, and the wider community.

## Honesty and transparency

We communicate openly and truthfully. We don't hide mistakes or sugarcoat difficult truths. When we make a commitment, we honor it. When we can't, we communicate early and clearly.

We believe transparency builds trust, and trust is the currency of great partnerships.

## Respect and dignity

We treat everyone with respect, regardless of their position, background, or beliefs. We value diversity and create an inclusive environment where everyone can thrive.

We don't tolerate discrimination, harassment, or behavior that undermines the dignity of others.

## Confidentiality

We respect the confidentiality of information entrusted to us by clients, partners, and colleagues. We safeguard sensitive information and only share it when authorized or legally required to do so.

What happens in client projects, stays in client projects.

## Conflicts of interest

We avoid situations where personal interests might conflict with our professional responsibilities. If a conflict arises, we disclose it immediately and work together to find an appropriate solution.

We prioritize the best interests of our clients and our company above personal gain.

## Quality and accountability

We take pride in our work and hold ourselves to the highest standards. We don't cut corners or deliver subpar work. If something isn't right, we fix it.

We own our mistakes, learn from them, and continuously improve.

## Fair dealing

We compete fairly and honestly in the marketplace. We don't misrepresent our capabilities, disparage competitors, or engage in deceptive practices.

We win work on merit, not manipulation.

## Compliance with laws

We comply with all applicable laws and regulations. We don't engage in illegal activities or help others do so.

When in doubt, we seek guidance.

## Speaking up

If you see behavior that violates this code, speak up. We encourage open dialogue and protect those who raise concerns in good faith.

Silence enables wrongdoing. Courage corrects it.

## Living our values

This Code of Ethics isn't just a document — it's a living commitment. We review it regularly, hold each other accountable, and continuously work to ensure our actions align with our values.

Because integrity isn't what we say. It's what we do.
