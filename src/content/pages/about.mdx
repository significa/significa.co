## How we work

Small teams, high ownership, no handoff walls. Designers code, engineers have opinions about UX, and everyone talks to clients. We believe the best work happens when disciplines overlap.

We're selective about the work we take on. We'd rather do fewer things exceptionally well than many things adequately.
