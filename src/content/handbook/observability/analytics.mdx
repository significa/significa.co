Often, developers may view analytics as more relevant to product and marketing, and we understand that it might not pique your interest. However, analytics play a vital role in product definition and design decisions, and our clients will be on our case about it.

The challenge lies in using analytics tools that are typically bloated and infringe on user privacy — something we actively avoid. At Significa, we use tools like PostHog and Plausible, focusing on understanding product usage rather than intrusive cross-site tracking.

If you would like to learn more about how we do this ourselves internally for our own website, head over to Website Analytics or Marketing at Significa for an even deeper dive.
