Diesta is unlocking a new level of premium processing, rethinking how money moves through the insurance industry.

When we pay for insurance — house, car, health, pet — that money bounces between brokers, intermediaries, and insurers, splitting into taxes, commissions, and premiums along the way. Behind the scenes, it's manual reconciliation and Excel spreadsheets at scale. Diesta's platform automates the entire process, reconciling payments in real time.

We helped them evolve their platform into something that could scale with their ambition: rethinking how the product worked, looked, and felt from the ground up.

## Rethinking the Diesta experience.

We workshopped and mapped our way through improvements, new features, and necessary tweaks, focusing on the platform's main flows.

We fixed interaction patterns — modals, clickable table rows, and expected behaviours — making complex processes feel manageable.
