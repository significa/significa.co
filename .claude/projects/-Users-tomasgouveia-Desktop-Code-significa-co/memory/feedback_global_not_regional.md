Never say Significa works in specific regions like "Europe and the US", "Europe", "the US", or any geographic subset of clients.

**Why:** Significa works across the globe — restricting to regions misrepresents the business and excludes potential clients.

**How to apply:** Use "worldwide", "globally", or "across the globe" when describing reach. "Scale-ups worldwide" or "ambitious companies globally" are acceptable. Never name specific continents or countries as the target market in copy or metadata.
