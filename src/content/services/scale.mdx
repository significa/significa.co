## Our approach.

We measure what matters, not what's easy to measure. Retention, task completion, conversion, support volume: the signals that tell you whether the product is actually working for the people who use it. Then we prioritise improvements by impact, not by what's fastest to ship.

## Why Significa.

We built it. We know where the complexity lives, what shortcuts were taken under deadline pressure, and what the original intent was behind every decision. That institutional knowledge compounds. A new team picking up someone else's codebase will spend weeks understanding what we already know. That's why long-term partnerships are more efficient than project-by-project engagements.
