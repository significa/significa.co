There are three exceptions to our Pricing model:

These exceptions are for:
- Registered NGOs.
- Companies that are certified members of 1% for the Planet or Planetmark.
- New or returning companies that come to Significa through our Client referral program.

## NGOs

For NGOs we partner with through our 1% for the Planet partnership, and depending on their needs, we may offer in-kind contributions through our varied range of services as part of our 1% commitment.

Service donations are valued at our normal billable rates.

For registered NGOs that we are not formally partnered with, we are pleased to be able to offer a discount of 25% for projects with a minimum estimated value of 60.000€.

We are also proud to offer pro-bono work for NGOs through which our team members volunteer as part of our Perks and benefits.

## 1% for the Planet members

Lastly, we are happy to offer fellow certified business members of 1% for the Planet a 10% discount on the pricing packages listed within their proposal.

You can learn more about 1% for the Planet, if you are interested in learning more about joining as a member or keen to learn about our perspective, feel free to get in touch.

## Client referral program

We have a full page for this. Off you go to Client referral program.

Please note that discounts coming through partnerships and our Client referral program do not accumulate. It's either-or in terms of which discount you claim.
